Uttarakhand's Joshimath Hit By 4.6 Magnitude Earthquake

An earthquake of magnitude 4.6 hits near Uttarakhand's Pauri Garhwal area, 31 km West-Southwest of Joshimath. According to India's National Center for Seismology (NCS), the tremors were felt 31 kilometers South-West of Joshimath at 5:58 am on Saturday. No loss of life and property has been reported yet.
